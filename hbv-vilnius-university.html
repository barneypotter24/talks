What is HBV?

  • Causative agent of heptatitis B
    • Chrrhosis
    • Hepatocellular carcinoma
  • Spread through blood and bodily fluids
  • Affects $\geq 350,000,000$ people annually
  • Partialy dsDNA genome
    • Long strand: 3 kb
    • Short strand: 2.2 kb
  • partially dsDNA genome
  • partially ciruclar
  • overlapping reading frames
Source: Morens & Fauci (2020)
  • Increasingly interconnected, population dense world leads to more EIDs
  • Range from isolated outbreaks (MRSA, MERS) to epidemics of regional scale (West African Ebola, Zika in the Americias) to pandemics of global scale (SARS-CoV-2)
  • As EIDs continue to increasingly affect global public health, we need to develop tools to characterize them and inform public health interventions in a timely manner
Source: Kocher et al. (2021), Science.
